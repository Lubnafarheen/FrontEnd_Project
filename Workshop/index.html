Welcome

Lexicon is Sweden's largest competence developer of companies and offers the widest range of 30 centers. For over 40 years, we have helped clients invest in their staff, always with the goal of exceeding expectations.

Labor Market

Since 1979, Lexicon has worked with developing and training companies. We are now taking the experience from what the companies want on to you who want to enter the labor Marketand be successful in your professional role.

Lexicon University Of Applied Sciences

Take the step out or further in working life through our vocational training courses adapted based on what the companies demand.
